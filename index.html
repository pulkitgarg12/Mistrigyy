Labourer

A laborer is a person engaged in physical work or manual labor, typically in the context of construction, agriculture, or other physically demanding occupations.

Constructor

A constructor in the construction industry is typically a company or an individual responsible for the planning, coordination, and execution of construction projects.

Electrician

An electrician is a skilled tradesperson who specializes in the installation, repair, and maintenance of electrical systems and equipment.

Cleaner

A cleaner is a person whose job involves cleaning and maintaining the cleanliness of various spaces.Their primary responsibility is to ensure that the assigned areas are kept tidy, sanitized, and free of dirt or clutter.

Carpenter

A carpenter is a skilled tradesperson who specializes in working with wood to construct, install, and repair structures and items made of wood or wood substitutes.
